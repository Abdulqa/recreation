Privacy Policy – Pakistan Prize Bond

Last updated: April 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Recreation Tech ("we", "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Pakistan Prize Bond app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we collect your email address and display name.
  • Profile Information: Full name, mobile number (Pakistani format), and city — provided voluntarily when setting up your profile.
  • Ad Content: When you post a buy/sell ad for prize bonds, we collect the ad details (denomination, price, description) and any image you upload.
  • Device Token: We collect your device's FCM token to deliver push notifications (draw results, ad status updates).
  • Log Data: IP address, device name, OS version, app version, and crash logs via Firebase Crashlytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate the prize bond checking and P2P marketplace features
  • To send push notifications about draw results and the status of your ads
  • To display your contact details to potential buyers/sellers (only if you choose to show them)
  • To improve the app via crash reporting and analytics

    • 3. Third-Party Services

    The app uses the following third-party services that may collect data under their own privacy policies:

    • Google Sign-In – authentication
    • Google AdMob (with Facebook Audience Network mediation) – advertising
    • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics – analytics and crash reporting
    • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) – push notifications
    • Google Play Services

    4. News Aggregation

    The app aggregates publicly available RSS news feeds (GEO, DAWN, ARY). We do not store any personal data in connection with news browsing. Notification preferences for news are entirely opt-in and can be changed in the app settings at any time.

    5. Data Retention

    Your account data is retained as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all personal data (profile, ads, images, device tokens) is permanently deleted from our servers within 7 days.

    6. Account Deletion

    You can delete your account. you can send a deletion request to: Delete Account

    7. Children's Privacy

    This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
